### Retrieve Card [GET /cards/{cardId}]
Retrieve the Card object by its `cardId`. 

---
+ Parameters 
    + cardId (string, required) - {{card.cardId}}

+ Request (application/json)
    + Headers
    
            Authorization: Bearer <YOUR_ACCESS_TOKEN>

+ Response 200

    + Attributes 
        + card (Card)
